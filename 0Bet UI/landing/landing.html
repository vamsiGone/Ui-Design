How to Play

Scratch & Win
Instant Gratification
  • Buy a scratch card of your choice.
  • Use your mouse or touchscreen to "scratch" off the hidden areas and reveal symbols or numbers.
  • Match the revealed symbols/numbers with the winning combinations to win prizes.
  • Prizes can include cash rewards, bonus points, or free scratch cards.
  • It's a quick and straightforward game that offers immediate results and excitement.
Lucky Numbers
A Classic Lottery Experience
  • Choose a set of numbers from a specified range, e.g., 1 to 50.
  • Purchase a ticket for the upcoming draw.
  • Watch the live or automated drawing to see if your numbers match the winning combination.
  • Prizes vary based on the number of matches, with the jackpot for a complete match.
  • Additional features can include multipliers and bonus draws, adding excitement to the game.
Bingo Bonanza
Social and Exciting Bingo Game
  • Purchase one or more bingo cards with a grid of numbers.
  • Join a scheduled game session with other players.
  • As numbers are called, mark them on your card.
  • Complete a specified pattern (e.g., a line, X, or full card) to win a prize.
  • Bingo offers social interaction through chat features and often includes progressive jackpots and themed games to keep players engaged.
